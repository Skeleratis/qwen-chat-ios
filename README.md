## ℹ️ About qwen-chat-ios

qwen-chat-ios is a native iPhone app that lets you chat with AI without needing the internet. It runs Alibaba's Qwen language models locally on your device using Apple’s MLX framework. This means all data stays on your phone, and you do not need to connect to any server to get answers.

The app supports text and images. You can ask questions about photos you take or select from your library. It shows the AI's reasoning steps so you understand how it thinks before giving the final answer. You can switch between smaller or larger Qwen models during chat and see how fast the AI replies.

## 🔎 Key Features

- **On-Device AI Chat:** Works fully offline on your iPhone  
- **Vision Support:** Ask questions about photos from your camera or gallery  
- **Thought Process Display:** See expandable blocks that reveal the AI’s reasoning steps  
- **Model Switching:** Toggle between Qwen 3.5 models (0.8B and 2B) anytime  
- **Streaming Responses:** Watch replies appear word by word and stop them whenever you want  
- **Performance Metrics:** Check tokens processed per second after each answer

## 🧩 How to Use qwen-chat-ios

### Starting a Chat

- Tap the chat input at the bottom of the screen.
- Type your question or message.
- Tap **Send**.

### Using Photos

- Tap the photo icon in the input box.
- Choose to take a new photo or select one from your library.
- Once added, ask questions about the image.

### Viewing AI's Thoughts

- If the AI shows `<think>` blocks, tap them to expand.
- Read the AI’s reasoning before the final answer.

### Switching Models

- Tap the model selector on the top right.
- Pick between the smaller (0.8B) or larger (2B) Qwen versions during a conversation.

### Stopping Responses

- While the AI types answers, tap the stop button to interrupt.
- This works in case you want to skip long replies.

### Checking Performance

- After each reply, see the tokens per second value below the message.
- This number shows how fast the AI processed your request.
